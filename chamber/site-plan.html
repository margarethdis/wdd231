Site Plan: San Pedro Sula Business Hub

Student: Margareth Ramos

Site Name

San Pedro Sula Business Hub

Reason: This name conveys a central point for commercial activity and professional networking, making it attractive for both local entrepreneurs and foreign investors.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to support the economic growth of San Pedro Sula by providing a digital directory of local businesses, announcing upcoming commercial events, and facilitating the membership process for the Chamber of Commerce.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: As a new business owner, how can I find the requirements and benefits of joining the Chamber of Commerce?

Scenario 2: As a citizen, where can I see the list of certified local businesses to hire a reliable service?

Color Scheme

Primary: #003366
Secondary: #FFD700
Accent: #F4F4F4

The Primary Color will be used for high-level navigation and headers. The Secondary Color is reserved for call-to-action buttons, while the Accent provides a clean background for content areas.
