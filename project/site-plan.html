Wiki Cat

A site plan for a website dedicated to cat education, care tips, and adoption awareness.

I chose this name because it reflects a space to learn more about cats and how to care for them with love. It is a place for cat lovers who want to give the best care to their feline friends.

Site Purpose

The Wiki Cat website is designed for cat lovers, new cat owners, and anyone interested in learning about cats. It provides helpful information on cat care, fun facts, popular breeds, and tips for living happily with feline companions.

Scenarios

  • What are the best ways to care for a new kitten?
  • How can I tell if my cat is healthy or needs a vet visit?

Color Scheme

Clay Brown: Used for headings and buttons.

Light Beige: Used for main text/background.

Warm Black: Used for accents and highlights.

Foggy Gray: Used for soft backgrounds and section contrast.

Typography

Primary font: Roboto — used for all body text to ensure readability.

Secondary font: Playfair Display — used for headings to give an elegant and friendly look.
